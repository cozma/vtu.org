The Virginia Tech Union would like to welcome you to Virginia Tech! We hope this section of our website will help you during your stay in Blacksburg. This website will provide you with venue information, campus visit information as well as information on Blacksburg and the surrounding community. The Virginia Tech Union was formed in 1969, stemming from the university's belief that there should be one main student organization on campus that brings social and educational entertainment to Virginia Tech. At that time, the VTU became a University Chartered Student Organization and has since been the largest student programming board on campus. VTU is run by and for students. Acts are selected and brought to campus by the work of student committees and its advisors. VTU strives to make each performer's appearance on campus the best it can be.

Burruss Auditorium

A 3000-seat, five-section auditorium with seating located on the same level. Wheelchair seating is located in the right-center section at the middle cross aisle. The auditorium is also equipped with infrared assistive listening devices. The wireless devices can be reserved by calling the box office at 540-231-5615.

Recital Salon

The Recital Salon is located on the 2nd floor in Squires Student Center. The venue is maintained by the music department at Tech. The Recital Salon is an acoustically designed hall with good sight lines and seating for 240. Wheelchair seating is located in the rear of the house.

Colonial Hall

650 seat theatre/classroom style facility located on the 2nd floor of Squires Student Center. Wheelchair seating is located at the front of the hall. Large area in front that can be used for speakers and different presentation installations. Rear space is also available.

Commonwealth Ballroom

The Commonwealth Ballroom is a multi-purpose facility with a large stage. Good for small concerts, banquets, dances, etc. Capacity varies from 450-1800 and the room can be divided into two sections approximately 2/3 + 1/3 of entire ballroom.

Old Dominion Ballroom

Smaller facility than the Commonwealth Ballroom with similar uses. Capacity varies from 150-500 depending on event type and room setup. Room is not dividable, but does have a balcony. The ballroom is located on the first floor just across from the Student Centers.

Haymarket Theatre

500 seat theatre located on the 2nd floor in Squires Student Center. Wheelchair seating is located in right of the house. Wireless assistive listening devices may be reserved by contacting the Ticket Office for any ticketed event beforehand. Entrances are located on every floor of the theater

Studio Theatre

The Studio Theatre is located on the 2nd floor in Squires Student Center and is managed by the Theater Department at Virginia Tech. The theatre is small (seating capacity 222), intimate, and has excellent sound and lighting. Wheelchair seating is located in the rear of the house.

Lyric Theatre

Since 1998, the Lyric has operated a not-for-profit movie theatre and community center in the heart of downtown Blacksburg, Virginia. They are located on College Avenue across the street from Virginia Tech's main campus. It has also been home to live music acts.
